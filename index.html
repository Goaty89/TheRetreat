We are a quiet and cozy little family run retreat located in the middle of the Sungai Lembing Town Kuantan , Pahang , Malaysia. Facing a lush forest reserve we enjoy great views, cool temperatures and great ambiance for relaxation and serenity.

We are a privately owned retreat and are not a commercial hotel. As such, visits are strictly for booked in guests only. We value our privacy and the privacy of our visiting guests.

CANCELLATION POLICY

All our bookings are confirmed by full payment and it is non-refundable

WHAT TIME IS CHECK-IN / CHECK-OUT?

Guests can check in from 2pm to 10pm and check out anytime before 12pm. Early check in will be subject to availability and late check-out may be arranged.

IS BREAKFAST INCLUDED?

All the prices stated are for the room rates only. Breakfast is not included. However guests are free to make their own coffee or tea at the tea counter. Mineral water will be provided as well. There are local restaurants nearby our retreat too.

IS THERE FREE WI-FI?

We do provide free wifi for our guests and the password will be given upon check in. You can also approach the workers anytime during your stay for the password.

IS THERE A COMMON PANTRY?

We do have a common outdoor pantry for our guests. Feel free to make your own coffee or tea. You can also bring your own food and drinks and mingle with fellow travellers. We provide filtered water as well.

WHERE CAN I PARK MY CAR?

We do have a mini car park for our guests. Public parking is also available near our retreat.

WHAT SHOULD I BRING?

  • Bring your own toiletries and towel. For all the bathrooms, shampoo and shower gel are provided. Towels can be provided upon request.
  • Bring mosquito/insect repellent.
  • Bring torchlight for those who are going for hiking (Panorama hill).
